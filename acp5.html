Pea curry

How to make peas curry?

Ingredients Main: 1 ½ cups green peas (fresh or frozen).

Base: 1 large onion (finely chopped), 2 medium tomatoes (pureed), and 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste.

Spices: 1 tsp cumin seeds, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, and ½ tsp garam masala.

Creamy Finish (Optional): 2 tbsp heavy cream or a paste made from 5–6 soaked cashews.

Garnish: Fresh coriander leaves and 1 tsp crushed kasuri methi (dried fenugreek).









Step-by-Step Instructions Prepare the Peas: If using fresh peas, boil them in salted water until tender (about 10 minutes) and drain. Frozen peas can be added directly later or thawed in warm water.

Sauté Aromatics: Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle. Add the onions and sauté until translucent or light golden brown.

Add Ginger-Garlic: Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute until the raw smell disappears.

Cook the Tomato Base: Pour in the tomato puree. Cook on medium heat until the moisture evaporates and the oil begins to separate from the sides.

Spices & Creaminess: Lower the heat and add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. If using cashew paste or yogurt, stir it in now to add richness.

Simmer: Add the green peas and about ¾ cup of water to reach your desired consistency. Cover and simmer for 5–8 minutes so the peas absorb the flavours.

Final Touch: Stir in the garam masala, kasuri methi, and heavy cream. Cook for another 2 minutes.

Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
